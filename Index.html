Capital Products
What's available · What you need to qualify
Merchant Cash Advance
Fast capital. High cost. Not a loan.
Funds in 24–48 hours. Repaid as a % of daily sales. Best when the capital directly generates revenue.
Min revenue: $10K/mo
Min credit: 500+
Time in biz: 3+ months
Read the guide →
Business Line of Credit
Draw it, repay it, draw again.
The most flexible capital product available. You only pay interest on what you've drawn. Apply before you need it.
Min revenue: $15K/mo
Min credit: 600+
Time in biz: 6+ months
Read the guide →
Equipment Financing
The equipment is the collateral.
Better rates, lower credit requirements, faster approval. If you need a vehicle, machine, or tool — this beats a generic loan almost every time.
Min credit: 580+
Approval: 24–48 hours
Terms: 3–7 years
Read the guide →
SBA Loans
Best terms. Longest timeline.
Government-backed loans up to $5M. Lower rates and longer terms than anything else available. 4–8 week process.
Min revenue: $100K/yr
Min credit: 680+
Time in biz: 2+ years
Read the guide →
